Data Dictionary - Getting and Cleaning Data Project

SubID: 
	ID of the test subject. There are a total of 30 subjects.

ACT: 
	Activity being performed by the subject. These are "Walking", "Walking Upstairs", "Walking Downstairs", "Sitting", "Standing", and "Laying". A total of six different activities.

BodyAcc:
	Body acceleration. Recorded in X, Y, Z dimensions as well as mean and standard deviation.
GravityAcc:
	Acceleration due to gravity. Recorded in X, Y, Z dimensions as well as mean and standard deviation.
BodyGyro:
	Angular Velocity. Recorded in X, Y, Z dimensions as well as mean and standard deviation.
